ABOUT HISHOT

HIMTI Bina Nusantara is organizing an annual event called HISHOT, which stands for HIMTI
Seminar-Workshop and Study Tour. The event is designed to enrich participants' knowledge
and experience, and this year's focus is on the theme of "ADVANCE: Aiming to Advance the World with AI".
By joining and participating in HISHOT 2023, the community is given an opportunity to receive
a better understanding of how Artificial Intelligence is used to advance the world today.

Of course, HISHOT is open to the public, so for those who are interested in our events you are eligible to join!
You can visit this [link] to register. If you are new, you can register as Binusian or Non-Binusian and fill in all of the required data to get the member account. After that, you can choose the event that you are interested in and participate in it. For further information, you can check our event guidebook for registration.
In HISHOT, you can get both experience and knowledge that will be difficult to find in another event. For Binusian, you will receive SAT points and for all of the participants will get an e-certificate.
Of course, you will get a free shuttle bus limited to the first 29 seats for Binusian from the Kemanggisan region.
